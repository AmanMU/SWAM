SWAM CHAT

Chatbot for Laptop & Repair Center Recommendation and Computer Accessory Identification

Visit

Research Scope

Abstract

In the age of computers, individuals used to look for computers through every avenue or ask for such assistance to find the ideal equipment based on the necessity. It is therefore important to have all the information under one roof. Hence, this novel approach have proposed a chatbot recommendation system for computers, accessories and repair centers which provides those information at ease. This particular method uses Large Language Models to understand the user’s preferences and requirements through a user-friendly Chat - Bot interface. Furthermore, this platform utilized a state of the art ASR model to gather audio inputs. This study tends to give the best solutions towards users by collecting reviews of devices and repair centers by web-scraping certain websites to gather relevant data. In addition, image processing has been introduced in order to identify computer accessories without any difficulties. All these features will be available through the proposed chatbot system such that any individual with varying knowledge of electronic devices can get a solution without any hesitation.

Research Gap

In relation to the literature surveys that were conducted, few research gaps were discovered and listed below:

  •  None of the existing systems combine similarity checking and OCR to identify computer hardware components.
  •  Sri Lankan websites utilize filtering and sorting. Unique features include extracting YouTube product reviews via speech-to-text for recommendations and voice-driven chatbot interactions.
  •  Not personalized & Lacks emotions - Most systems do not take the factor of user’s emotions into account. Thus, making it difficult to give more personalized results to users.
  •  Limited Functionality – One of the major issues that Chat-Bot systems are facing is finding it difficult to understand multi – part questions.

Research Problem

Objectives

  • Increase user satisfaction and cater their needs. ​
  • Determined user needs by a Chat-Bot system using NLP and image processing techniques.
  •  Recommender system to suggest devices & repair centers based on online reviews & product specification.​

Methodology

Image Classification

  • A dataset consisting of around 10000 images of computer hardware was created.
  • Using VGG16 pre-trained model features of the images were extracted.
  • Using OCR most common keywords were extracted from the images in the dataset.
  • When a new image is uploaded to the system, the features of the image are extracted and compared with the features of the images in the dataset.
  • The most similar image is retrieved and the keywords of the image are extracted using OCR.
  • Image is classified by combining the feature similarity and OCR results.

Chat bot with NER and Intent Classifier

  • Building a State Based Chat - Bot as the major interface where users can interact.
  • Intent Classifier Model to identify users' intents and identify the state based on the intent.
  • Name Entity Recognition Model (NER) to identify the entities within the queries and pass the specifications towards the recommendation model.

Automatic Speech Recognition

  • ASR technology in Sri Lankan websites enhances product searches by converting speech to text, integrating YouTube video reviews, and enabling visual product searches.
  • Two primary ASR development approaches were considered: CTC Architecture using the LJ Speech dataset and the Seq2Seq Approach with the "Common Accent" dataset, ultimately opting for Seq2Seq for its regional accent recognition capabilities.
  • The chosen Seq2Seq method employs Mel-spectrogram for acoustic analysis and 30-second audio chunks for model training, ensuring accurate transcription of audio into text utilizing an encoder and decoder with cross-attention mechanisms, significantly improves ASR system accuracy and enhances the user experience in Sri Lanka.

Laptop & Repair Center Recommendation

  • Employs collaborative and content-based recommendation models.
  • Generates TFIDF vector from user's keywords, compares it with the laptop data set using cosine similarity, and suggests similar laptops.
  • Integrates endorsements from users with similar typologies to enhance recommendations.
  • Identifies repair centers within a 10-kilometer radius based on the user's location.
  • Utilizes Google reviews for evaluation, enabling users to choose repair centers with higher ratings and credibility.
  • Provides essential details such as contact numbers, locations, and ratings for selected repair centers.
